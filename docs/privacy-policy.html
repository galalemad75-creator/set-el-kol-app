Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026
App: ست الكل — Set El Kol

Introduction

Set El Kol ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

  • No account required: You can use the app without creating an account or providing any personal details.
  • No analytics: We do not use any analytics services, tracking tools, or advertising SDKs.
  • No cookies: The app does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.
  • No location data: We do not access or collect your location data.

Third-Party Services

The app uses TheMealDB API (www.themealdb.com) to fetch recipe data. When you search for or view recipes, requests are made to TheMealDB's servers. These requests may include:

  • Your search query (recipe name or ingredient)
  • Your IP address (transmitted automatically by your device)

We do not control TheMealDB's data practices. Please refer to TheMealDB's own privacy policy for information about how they handle data.

Data Stored Locally

The app stores the following data locally on your device using localStorage:

  • Favorite recipes: A list of recipes you've saved as favorites
  • Language preference: Your chosen language (Arabic, English, or French)
  • Theme preference: Your chosen display mode (light or dark)

This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time using the "Clear Cache" option in Settings, or by uninstalling the app.

Offline Functionality

The app uses a Service Worker to cache recipe data for offline access. Cached data is stored locally in your browser's cache and can be cleared through the app settings or your device's app settings.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our app is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not contain any inappropriate content, advertising, or social features.

Data Protection (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we act as the data controller. Since we do not collect personal data, GDPR data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability) are not applicable in the traditional sense. However:

  • All locally stored data can be cleared by the user at any time
  • No data is transmitted to our servers (we have no servers)
  • Uninstalling the app removes all local data

Security

All API communications with TheMealDB are conducted over HTTPS, ensuring that data in transit is encrypted. However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
